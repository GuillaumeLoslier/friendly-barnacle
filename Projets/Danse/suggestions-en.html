Group



This project took several forms. First, I wrote a book with 365 suggestions for dancing in different ways, for example, "Dance as if you were surrounded by bubbles that you have to pop in order to move".
Then, for a period of one year, I created one dance a day that was an improvised response to each of these suggestions, dancing all over the island of Montreal, outside. I filmed these dances to make short videos, shared on social media.
I invited about twenty dancers to participate in my process, to improvise their own response to these suggestions, and I filmed these dances. The concept has been taken up by several dancers and dance teachers around the world, from Germany to Alaska.
