Hostile Architecture

In 2018, I was invited to create a work at the théâtre aux Écuries in an event for emerging artists organized by La Serre. There were several artists, and each could choose the studio where they would perform. I chose to dance in a more industrial looking room with the trash cans.

No Need for Blue Jeans Here

I worked at a recycling center for several years and was amazed at the tons of materials we waste as a society. I created a piece with Aliah Schwartz (choreographer) and Darah Mia (set and lighting designer) that combines dance and object theater, using garbage and dead flowers as sets and costumes. The show was presented at Tangente (Montreal), Kinetic Studio (Halifax) and the ZH Festival (Montreal).
