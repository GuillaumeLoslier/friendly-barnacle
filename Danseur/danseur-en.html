Here are some of the projects in which I had the chance to participate as a dancer.

By His Loving Wife (2022)

Créations Élise Legrand

A surreal and festive celebration fluctuating between the absurd and the poetic.

Carte Postale (2017-2018)

Casson & Friends

A piece inspired by a town (Ipswich and Montreal), its inhabitants and their memories.

I Stand In (2017)

Julie Vulcan

A durational performance in honour of the forgotten, misplaced, unrecovered and removed

Utility Kid (2016)

Isabella Leone

A performance installation and inquiry into economic utility

D'amour, ils se gaveront... (2015)

Camille L.-Wilsey

Duo of comic movements inspired by violence and seduction.

Shé Wèrè (2015)

Tobi Ayedadjou

Collective performance on madness.

Où est Julio (2015)

Je suis Julio

Poetic manifesto without slogans or symbols.
