Why do I dance?

  1. Dancing brings me joy and it is a joy I would like to share. That's why I started dancing.
  2. Dance allows us to have a kinesthetic intelligence through a creative and curious relationship with the body, which we too often see as a burden or a vessel for the brain.
  3. Contemporary art, including contemporary dance, helps us to re-evaluate, feel and taste again the world in which we are situated.
  4. Contemporary dance as I see it is not a closed vessel but a communicating vessel, a way of thinking in movement that is inspired and mixed with all other forms of knowledge.
